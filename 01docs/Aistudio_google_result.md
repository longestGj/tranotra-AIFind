1.  **TBS Group (Synthetic Leather/Footwear):** As one of Vietnam’s largest manufacturing giants partnering with brands like Skechers and Coach, their demand for PVC plasticizers for synthetic leather is massive. 3.  **VN EcoFloor (LVT/SPC Flooring):** LVT (Luxury Vinyl Tile) production is extremely plasticizer-intensive. Since they export hundreds of containers monthly to the USA and Germany, they are likely searching for stable, REACH-compliant **DOTP** suppliers in China to maintain their competitive pricing.
